About Me

I am an Associate Architect and Assistant Principal Engineer at Samsung Electronics with over 14 years of experience in mobile and wearables software development. I have a strong background in building robust and maintainable systems. I have made significant contributions to the Samsung Health for iOS app, which has over 6 million users. I have also filed seven patent applications, one of which has been granted in multiple countries.

I currently lead the Health Sensing Modalities Team, where I focus on AI-driven health sensing and on-device intelligence for wearables. My work includes developing a stress detection algorithm, a non-invasive sweat sensor for cholesterol and glucose detection, and a system for estimating Dim Light Melatonin Onset. I have also led the development of foundation models for hypertension and PVC detection using PPG sensor data.

My technical skills include Digital Health, Android and iOS app development, Wearables, System Design, and Application Architecture. I am also interested in AI, Technological Innovation, and the Philosophy of Science.
