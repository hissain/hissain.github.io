About Me

I am an Associate Architect and Assistant Principal Engineer at Samsung Electronics with over 14 years of experience in mobile and wearables software development. I lead the Health Sensing Modalities Team, focusing on AI-driven health sensing and on-device intelligence. My work currently involves developing advanced algorithms for stress detection, non-invasive blood analysis, and foundation models for physiological signals analysis.

Previously, I made significant contributions to the Samsung Health iOS app (6M+ users) and hold multiple patents in wearable technology. I am passionate about Digital Health, System Architecture, and the intersection of AI and daily life.
