About Me

I am an Associate Architect and Assistant Principal Engineer at Samsung Electronics with over 14 years of experience in Mobile and Wearables. I specialize in building robust, performant systems and have contributed significantly to Samsung Health for iOS (6M+ users). My work includes seven patent applications, with one granted.

Currently, I lead the Health Sensing Modalities Team, pioneering AI-driven health sensing and on-device intelligence. Key innovations include stress detection algorithms for earbuds, non-invasive sweat sensors (with Caltech), and DLMO estimation (with MIT). I also led the development of on-device foundation models for blood pressure and heart health monitoring and conducted clinical studies on women's health.

My expertise spans Digital Health, Mobile development, System Design, and BLE. Beyond tech, I am passionate about AI, physics, philosophy, and music, viewing software engineering as a means to solve real-world problems and improve human well-being.
